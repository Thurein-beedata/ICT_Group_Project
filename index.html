English

What is English in IGCSE?

IGCSE English helps students develop strong reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. It teaches learners how to communicate clearly, accurately, and confidently in different situations. Students study a variety of texts and learn how language is used to inform, describe, and persuade.

What do students learn in English?

Students learn how to analyse passages, understand meaning, identify writers’ techniques, and express ideas in well-structured writing. They also improve grammar, vocabulary, spelling, and punctuation. In some courses, students may practise summary writing, speech writing, and directed writing.

Why is English important?

English is important because it supports success in many other subjects and in daily life. It helps students become better readers, better writers, and stronger communicators. These skills are useful for further study, future careers, and effective communication in the modern world.

Mathematics

What is Mathematics in IGCSE?

IGCSE Mathematics develops students’ numerical, logical, and problem-solving skills. It teaches learners how to work with numbers, formulas, shapes, graphs, and data in a clear and systematic way. Mathematics is a core subject that provides a strong foundation for science, business, and technology-related fields.

What do students learn in Mathematics?

Students learn topics such as algebra, geometry, trigonometry, statistics, probability, ratios, and equations. They practise solving problems step by step and applying mathematical methods to real-life situations. They also learn how to interpret graphs, calculate measurements, and use logical reasoning accurately.

Why is Mathematics important?

Mathematics is important because it helps students think critically and solve problems efficiently. It is widely used in everyday life, from budgeting and shopping to understanding data and measurements. It is also essential for careers in engineering, finance, computing, architecture, and many scientific professions.

Science

What is Science in IGCSE?

IGCSE Science introduces students to the study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and explanation. It helps learners understand how living things, materials, forces, and energy behave. Science encourages curiosity and teaches students how evidence is used to explain everyday phenomena.

What do students learn in Science?

Students explore topics from Biology, Chemistry, and Physics depending on the course they take. They may study cells, the human body, chemical reactions, electricity, energy, motion, and the environment. They also learn practical skills such as planning experiments, recording results, and drawing scientific conclusions.

Why is Science important?

Science is important because it helps students understand the world around them and how things work. It develops analytical thinking, practical investigation skills, and the ability to evaluate evidence. Science is also a valuable foundation for careers in medicine, engineering, research, and environmental studies.

Business Studies

What is Business Studies in IGCSE?

IGCSE Business Studies introduces students to the world of business and entrepreneurship. It helps learners understand how businesses are organised, managed, and operated in local and global markets. Students explore how companies make decisions, compete, and respond to customer needs.

What do students learn in Business Studies?

Students learn about business ownership, marketing, production, finance, human resources, and operations. They also study how businesses grow, manage costs, recruit staff, and make strategic decisions. Real-world case studies are often used to help students apply their knowledge to practical business situations.

Why is Business Studies important?

Business Studies is important because it develops decision-making, communication, and problem-solving skills. It helps students understand how businesses affect the economy and daily life. The subject is useful for learners who are interested in entrepreneurship, management, marketing, or finance.

Economics

What is Economics in IGCSE?

IGCSE Economics helps students understand how individuals, businesses, and governments make choices about limited resources. It explores how economies work and how decisions affect production, consumption, employment, and living standards.

What do students learn in Economics?

Students learn about supply and demand, opportunity cost, market systems, inflation, unemployment, international trade, and government policies. They also examine how prices are set and how economic decisions influence households, firms, and entire countries.

Why is Economics important?

Economics is important because it helps students understand real-world issues such as poverty, rising prices, taxation, and trade. It builds analytical skills and encourages logical thinking about money, choices, and public policies. It is especially useful for students interested in business, finance, politics, or social studies.

Accounting

What is Accounting in IGCSE?

IGCSE Accounting teaches students how financial information is recorded, organised, and interpreted. It helps learners understand how businesses track income, expenses, assets, and profits. Accounting is an essential subject for understanding how organisations manage money responsibly.

What do students learn in Accounting?

Students learn bookkeeping, ledger accounts, trial balances, income statements, and statements of financial position. They also study cash books, depreciation, corrections of errors, and the interpretation of financial records. Accuracy, organisation, and logical working are key parts of the subject.

Why is Accounting important?

Accounting is important because it helps students develop financial awareness and attention to detail. It supports understanding of how businesses measure performance and make financial decisions. The subject is useful for future study and careers in accounting, auditing, banking, and business management.

ICT

What is ICT in IGCSE?

IGCSE ICT focuses on the use of technology to create, process, store, and communicate information. It teaches students how digital tools are used in education, business, and everyday life. The subject combines practical computer skills with an understanding of how technology affects society.

What do students learn in ICT?

Students learn how to use word processing, spreadsheets, databases, presentations, and web-based tools. They also study topics such as data security, communication systems, file management, and the ethical use of technology. Many tasks involve practical work on computers as well as theory-based understanding.

Why is ICT important?

ICT is important because digital skills are needed in almost every modern career. It helps students work efficiently, organise information, and use technology responsibly. The subject prepares learners for further study and for a world where computers and digital communication are essential.

Computer Science

What is Computer Science in IGCSE?

IGCSE Computer Science helps students understand how computers work and how software is created. It introduces programming, problem-solving, and the principles behind computer systems. The subject is designed for learners who want to explore the logic and structure of computing.

What do students learn in Computer Science?

Students study programming concepts such as variables, loops, conditions, arrays, and algorithms. They also learn about data representation, hardware, software, networks, cybersecurity, and logic. In many parts of the course, students apply their knowledge by designing and writing simple programs.

Why is Computer Science important?

Computer Science is important because it develops logical thinking, creativity, and technical problem-solving skills. It helps students understand the technology that powers modern systems and applications. It is an excellent foundation for careers in software development, data science, artificial intelligence, and engineering.

Geography

What is Geography in IGCSE?

IGCSE Geography helps students understand the relationship between people, places, and the environment. It explores both physical processes, such as rivers and weather, and human issues, such as population and urban growth. Geography encourages learners to think about the world at local, regional, and global levels.

What do students learn in Geography?

Students study topics such as climate, natural hazards, ecosystems, settlement, migration, and development. They also learn map skills, data interpretation, and fieldwork techniques. The subject often involves analysing case studies and understanding how environmental and human systems are connected.

Why is Geography important?

Geography is important because it helps students understand major global challenges such as climate change, resource management, and urbanisation. It develops research, analysis, and decision-making skills. It is valuable for students interested in environmental science, travel, planning, and international development.

History

What is History in IGCSE?

IGCSE History helps students understand important events, people, and changes from the past. It teaches learners how societies developed and how historical events continue to influence the present. The subject encourages critical thinking through the study of evidence, causes, consequences, and interpretations.

What do students learn in History?

Students study selected historical periods, major conflicts, international relations, and social change. They learn how to examine sources, compare viewpoints, and build reasoned arguments using historical evidence. Topics vary by syllabus, but often include world wars, political change, and international developments.

Why is History important?

History is important because it helps students understand how the modern world was shaped. It develops research, writing, evaluation, and argument skills. The subject is useful for students interested in law, politics, journalism, education, and the humanities.

Art & Design

What is Art & Design in IGCSE?

IGCSE Art & Design allows students to express ideas creatively through visual work. It helps learners explore drawing, painting, design, and other artistic processes while developing technical and imaginative skills. The subject encourages observation, experimentation, and personal expression.

What do students learn in Art & Design?

Students learn how to use different materials, techniques, and visual elements to create original work. They may explore colour, composition, texture, perspective, and research into artists’ styles. The course often includes coursework and project development from initial ideas to final outcomes.

Why is Art & Design important?

Art & Design is important because it develops creativity, observation, and independent thinking. It helps students communicate visually and build confidence in presenting original ideas. The subject is valuable for those interested in design, architecture, media, fashion, and the creative industries.

French

What is French in IGCSE?

IGCSE French helps students learn to communicate in one of the world’s most widely spoken languages. It develops practical language skills for everyday situations, travel, study, and cultural understanding. Students improve their confidence in using French in both spoken and written forms.

What do students learn in French?

Students learn vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and sentence structure in French. They practise listening, speaking, reading, and writing through common topics such as school, family, hobbies, travel, and future plans. The course also helps learners understand aspects of French-speaking cultures.

Why is French important?

French is important because learning another language improves communication skills and cultural awareness. It can open opportunities for travel, international study, and employment. It also supports memory, confidence, and the ability to understand people from different backgrounds.

Arabic

What is Arabic in IGCSE?

IGCSE Arabic helps students develop the ability to understand and use Arabic in meaningful contexts. It supports communication skills, reading comprehension, and writing accuracy while building knowledge of the language and its culture. The course is suitable for students who want to strengthen their Arabic for academic or practical use.

What do students learn in Arabic?

Students learn vocabulary, grammar, sentence structure, reading techniques, and writing skills in Arabic. They practise understanding texts, expressing opinions, and responding to different language tasks. Depending on the syllabus, they may also develop listening and speaking skills for real-life communication.

Why is Arabic important?

Arabic is important because it is one of the major international languages and is widely used across many countries. Learning Arabic helps students connect with culture, history, and communication in the Arab world. It is valuable for future study, travel, business, and cross-cultural understanding.

Physical Education

What is Physical Education in IGCSE?

IGCSE Physical Education combines knowledge about sport, fitness, and the human body with practical performance. It helps students understand the importance of health, exercise, and active lifestyles. The subject includes both theoretical learning and physical participation.

What do students learn in Physical Education?

Students learn about anatomy, physiology, training methods, fitness, nutrition, and the rules and strategies of sports. They may also study motivation, skill development, and factors that affect performance. In the practical part of the course, students demonstrate their abilities in selected physical activities.

Why is Physical Education important?

Physical Education is important because it promotes healthy habits, teamwork, discipline, and self-improvement. It helps students understand how exercise affects the body and mind. The subject is useful for learners interested in sports science, coaching, physiotherapy, health, and fitness careers.
